About Elvina

Elvina started with a simpel idea. 

Elvina stands for fairness. We believe that our workers who make the shoes, ELVINA Fair Footwear, deserve a fair pay for their hard work. We want better working conditions, fair and equal pay for all of our employees. We offer our employees trainings so that they can extend their knowledge and abilities

Elvina started with a simple idea and we can't wait to finally show you our life work.

Vision 

In a world of uncertainty and chaos, we believe you should not fear the unknown because only then you will be bold enough to make a change.

Mission 

At Elvina we aspire to set an example for employers all around the world.

We dream of a fair world where people are excited and happy to work.

Elvina wants to help invest in a better world: Reduce, Reuse, Recycle.

Our team

Our founders are three exceptional women that wanted two things; equality and a better world for future generations to care for.
